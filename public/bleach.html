ABOUT BLEACH:

Bleach is an anime series created by Tite Kubo that originated from manga in August 7, 2001. It features Ichigo Kurosaki as the main character and his friends: Orihime Inoue, Uryu Ishida, Sado "Chad" Yasutora, Rukia Kuchiki, and Renji Abarai. Ichigo obtained the powers of a Soul Reaper by borrowing the powers (and soon awakening his') of Rukia. He uses this new power to protect his family and friends from the dangers of his enemies.
