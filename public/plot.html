You are an exchange student in Daikoku Hearts Private Academy and you were chosen as one of the students who will participate in the exchange program alongside Light Yagami and Misa Amane. The story takes place in Paper Hearts Highschool.

The game is intended to be at Otome game, where Light Yagami and Ichiro Kurosaki are the main male love interests. Meanwhile, Misa Amane and Orihime are the female love interests. However, Misa will start hating you if you end up getting too close with Light, since she herself is also into him. There is also supposed to be secret endings, which will introduce different love interests, aside from the main ones.
